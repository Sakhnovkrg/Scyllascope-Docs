# Hold & Reset

![Hold & Reset](/images/10.webp)

## Hold

Captures the current waveform state and keeps it as a background reference. While Hold is active, the live waveform continues to draw on top, so you can compare before and after â€” for example, to see how a compressor or EQ changes the signal shape. Turning Hold off clears the captured waveform.

## Reset

Clears the live waveform buffer. Does not affect the Hold snapshot.
