# Division Selector

![Division Selector](/images/06.webp)

Controls the grid size and the visible time range of the waveform. In [Sync](/header/mode-selector#sync) mode the grid aligns to bars, in [Free](/header/mode-selector#free) mode it determines how much audio fits on screen. Available divisions: **1/4**, **1/2**, **1**, **2**, **4**, and **8** bars.

The **T** button on the right switches the grid to triplet subdivisions.
