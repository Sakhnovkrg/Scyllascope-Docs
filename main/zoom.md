# Zoom & Navigation

![Zoom & Navigation](/images/12.gif)

You can zoom all the way down to individual samples — there is no limit.

## Controls

- [[Mouse Wheel]] — zoom in/out at the cursor position.
- **Left Click + Drag** — zoom and pan in one gesture.
- [[Shift]] + [[Mouse Wheel]] — scroll horizontally.
- **Scrollbar edges** — drag the left or right edge of the scrollbar to zoom from that side.
- **Scrollbar body** — drag to pan across the waveform.
